Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year round. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik. Add even more adventure to you stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing glacier with us.

Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier, only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in contact with the real reason behind the name Ice-land!
